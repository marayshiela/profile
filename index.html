My Skills.

Traditional and Digital Drawing

When I was a kid, I became fascinated by art. After seeing some of my brother's drawings, I told myself that I really wanted to draw. Drawing becomes an extension of myself. I used to scribble on the backs of my notebooks when I was a student, and I spent most of my summers doing art.


Traditional art mediums that I frequently use include sketching, acrylic, watercolor, and scribble art. But after discovering vector art, I decided to do some digital art as well. It's great to find new ways to do things. It can really motivate you to do more.

My Artwork

I keep an Art Journal in which I draw people, places, or images that interest me, and I occasionally paint acrylic paintings on canvas. The majority of my art journal entries are also shared on Instagram.
